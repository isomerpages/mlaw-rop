<p>Dear licensee,</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Financial Action Task Force (“FATF”) published a peer evaluation report
of Singapore, which strongly affirmed that Singapore has a robust and effective
framework and process to counter money-laundering (“ML”), terrorism financing
(“TF”) and proliferation financing (“PF”).</p>
<p></p>
<p>You may refer to the press release jointly published by the Monetary Authority
of Singapore (“MAS”), the Ministry of Home Affairs (“MHA”), and the Ministry
of Finance (“MOF”).</p>
<p></p>
<p>The published report is available on the FATF website. The report has
also been uploaded at the ROP website.</p>
<p></p>
<p>The fight against ML/TF/PF is a shared responsibility. We thank you for
your continued support and partnership to keep Singapore’s AML/CFT/CPF
regime strong and trusted.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Thank you.</p>
